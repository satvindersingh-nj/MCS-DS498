Manchester United
The Manchester United Football Club was founded in 1878 as Newton Heath LYR Football Club and was renamed to Manchester United in 1902. In 1910 Manchester United moved to Old Trafford in Manchester. It is one of the most popular football clubs in the world both in terms of attendance and in terms of earnings with an annual revenue of over US$ 796.4 million in 2019.
