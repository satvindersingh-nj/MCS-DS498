Manchester United
The Manchester United Football Club have won more trophies than any other professional club in English Football with a total of 20 Premier League Titles, 12 FA Cups, 5 League Cups and a record 21 FA Community Shields. Founded in 1878 as Newton Heath LYR Football Club was renamed to Manchester United in 1902 and moved to Old Trafford, Manchester, UK in 1910. It is one of the most popular football clubs in the world both in terms of attendance and in terms of earnings with an annual revenue of over US$ 796.4 million in 2019.
